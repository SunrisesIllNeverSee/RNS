# PPA Addendum: Contextual Token SNR Classification

Claim: A method for classifying individual language tokens (words, subwords, or characters)
as signal or noise based not on their identity but their functional role within a given context.

This system enables:
- Dynamic assignment of signal/noise to tokens across time
- Measurement of signal compression and drift
- Upstream validation of token utility via diagnostic output

This method powers downstream systems including leaderboard scoring, resonance logging, and signal vault indexing.
