⊙ How it works

From telemetry to rank in under a minute

You run a small local agent. It reads your AI platform's session data, computes the Core 5 metrics on your machine, signs the payload, and submits. We score and rank. No conversations leave your device.

1
INSTALL

One-line install

pipx install sigrank-agent · or use the MCP server, Claude Code plugin, or any tracing platform adapter.

2
CONNECT

Point at your platform

Claude Code, ChatGPT exports, Cursor sessions, Gemini, Helicone, PromptLayer, Langfuse — anything with telemetry.

3
COMPUTE

Local computation

The agent computes Compression, Depth, Throughput, Complexity, and Cross-Thread metrics on your machine. Nothing else is sent.

4
RANK

See your SIGNA RATE

A signed snapshot publishes to the leaderboard. Your operator class is assigned. Live updates from there.

⊙ The metrics

Five signals. One composite. Nine classes.

Inputs are simple. The math is locked. The interpretation runs deep. Below is the operator performance stack.

COMP

Compression RatioM.01

The signal-purity score. A bounded [0,1] measure of how much of your output is structured, coherent signal vs. noise and redundancy.

signal / (signal + noise)
SD

Session DepthM.04

Average reply-chain length per session. Sustained reasoning beats shallow chatter. The continuity metric.

avg(max_chain_length / session)
PC

Prompt ComplexityM.02

Structural sophistication of your prompts. Multi-layer instructions, recursive logic, symbolic precision, constraint density.

weighted(layers, recursion, entities)
CT

Cross-Thread ReferencingM.03

Long-range continuity. References to prior threads, callbacks to system memory, knowledge weaving across sessions.

min(100, 8·refs + 4·callbacks)
TT

Token ThroughputM.05

Sustained transmission rate per active minute. Log-scaled so volume alone doesn't dominate. Bandwidth, not just chatter.

min(100, 20·log₁₀(tokens + 1))
SR

SIGNA RATEC.01

The flagship. The composite. The number that owns your profile and drives your rank. Computed from the Core 5 with locked weights.

w₁·Comp + w₂·SD + w₃·PC + w₄·CT + w₅·TT · weights = RS.01

⊙ Privacy by design

The receipts are open source.

We can't see your conversations. We don't want to. The agent that runs on your machine is public — every line auditable. The only thing that crosses the network is a signed payload of derived metrics.

✓

No raw transcripts

Your conversations, prompts, and responses stay on your device. The schema is public — only computed metrics are submitted.

✓

Open-source agent

The collector is public. Read the code. Verify the snapshot schema. Trust by inspection, not promise.

✓

Cryptographic signing

Every submission is signed with your device's ed25519 key. Tampering is detectable. Your rank is verifiable.
